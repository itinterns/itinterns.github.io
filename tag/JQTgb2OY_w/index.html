ITIP: How to Apply (AUC1920)

2019-08-21 #Application #SPC1920
Click for application form If you are not using a Nottingham-CN account, please contact itactivity@nottingham.edu.cn for assistance as the application form is not available to you. Benefits Benefits provided to interns may include: Salary Professional Training from IT Services Staff Certificate of Internship End-of-year Awards Teamwork and Project Experience Available Opportunities We are currently offering these opportunities: Parallel Lab, Developer (FoSE only) Job Description: With the latest technology, Parallel Lab research and develop projects benefitting the university community. Media & Public Relations, Intern Job Description: Rapid Response to ongoing IT-related incidents Weekly news & information article Organization fo events Coordination with on-campus clubs, organizations and offices IT Services Desk+, Intern Job Description: Providing professional peer-to-peer technical support to university staff and students. Salary Your salary would be CNY 20 per hour and is issued on a monthly basis. Personal Income Tax deducted when salary is above CNY 1,000 in a single issue. Application Procedures To complete your online application, you should email us your resume at itactivity@nottingham.edu.cn and fill out the application form with your university ID. Both shall be completed by 20 September 2019. You may find the link to form at bottom of the page. Please do not provide any extra supporting material as they will be ignored. Note that Android users may not be able to access the form via WeChat's built-in browser. Google Chrome is advised as a solution if this problem occurs on your mobile.
